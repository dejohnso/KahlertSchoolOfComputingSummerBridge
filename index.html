Bridge Program Information

The Summer Bridge Program is for incoming first-year students planning on majoring in a degree offered by the Kahlert School of Computing.

In the Bridge Program, participants will:

  • develop skills to be successful in college courses
  • meet other incoming students and form study groups
  • learn basic Python programming to get a head start in comuting courses
  • gain appreciation for computing topics and career options

Apply through this short online form.

Applications submitted by July 1, 2023 will be given full consideration. Participants will be selected based on the expected impact and utility of the bridge program to the applicant.

Contact Prof. David Johnson (dejohnso@cs.utah.edu) for additional information.

Who

The Summer Bridge Program is for incoming first-year students planning on majoring in a degree offered by the Kahlert School of Computing (Computer Science, Data Science, Software Development, or Computer Engineering).

Where & When

The bridge program runs for two weeks M-F from 10am-3pm during July 31-August 11 on the University of Utah campus. Housing is not provided.

Support

Participants will receive a new laptop computer suitable for computing coursework. Lunch is provided. Local transportation support with a bus pass is possible.
